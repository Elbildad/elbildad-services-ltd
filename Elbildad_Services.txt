About Elbildad Services
Elbildad Services LTD is a fast-growing China-to-Nigeria procurement, sourcing, and
business development company that makes global trade simple, reliable, and profitable for
Nigerian businesses and individuals.
Founded by Muhammad Elbildad Isah, we serve as a trusted bridge between high-quality
Chinese manufacturers and the Nigerian market. Whether you need vehicles, Electric
Vehicles (EVs), electronics, machinery, industrial equipment, or any other product, we
handle the entire process so you don’t have to.
We started with a clear mission: to drive Nigeria’s industrial and business growth by
providing world-class sourcing solutions at factory prices — without the usual headaches of
language barriers, unreliable suppliers, complicated payments, risky shipping, or regulatory
challenges.
With our physical office in Kano (No. 117 Unguwar Wambai, Inna Plaza, Gwarzo Road), we
combine digital efficiency with real local support. This presence allows us to offer
personalized service and build long-term trust, especially for larger or more complex
transactions.

Our Services
At Elbildad Services LTD, we deliver a complete suite of solutions that go far beyond basic
sourcing. We support every stage of your China-Nigeria trade journey — from product
acquisition to strategic business growth.
Core Procurement & Sourcing
We specialize in sourcing high-quality products directly from trusted Chinese
manufacturers and platforms (including 1688 and Alibaba). We do your need and manage
supplier matching, negotiation, quality inspection, consolidation, and door-to-door delivery
to Nigeria. Our transparent service fee model ensures you get factory prices with full
visibility and no hidden charges.
Supplier Vetting & Verification
We protect your investment through thorough supplier audits and verification. We conduct
detailed checks on your chosen or recommended suppliers — including background
verification, production capacity assessment, and quality assurance (with factory visits
where possible) — so you can trade confidently and minimize risks on larger transactions.
Cross-Border Payments
We simplify international transactions by handling secure payments in CNY (RMB), USD,
or HKD. You pay us comfortably in Naira, and we manage the currency conversion, banking

compliance, and transfer process — removing the stress of dealing directly with Chinese
payment systems.
Visa Processing & Study in China
Planning a business trip or educational opportunity in China? We provide professional
assistance with visa processing and guidance for studying in China, making it easier for you
to build direct connections, gain market knowledge, and strengthen your business network.
Business Development & Export Support
We help Nigerian exporters find reliable buyers in China and other international markets.
Our business modeling and development services also support entrepreneurs and
companies looking to optimize operations, improve efficiency, scale their import/export
activities, or refine their overall business strategy for sustainable growth in the global
market.
From reliable product sourcing and secure logistics to strategic business support and
market access, Elbildad Services is your trusted one-stop partner for successful ChinaNigeria trade.
